# The Assay Bestiary
*A catalog of database horrors, sins, and creatures.*
*"SHOULD HAVE RUN ASSAY."*

---

## Art Styles

- **Imagen Portrait Series** — dark flat vector creature illustrations. Iconic. Sticker/T-shirt material.
- **Wrightson/EC Comics** — dense pen and ink crosshatching. Organic horror. Hand-drawn and alive in a wrong way.
- **Sin City (Frank Miller)** — stark black and white, hard geometry, selective color. Noir dread.
- **Robert Crumb / Underground Comix** — grotesque caricature. The human sins. Uncomfortable truths.
- **God of War** — burnt orange, crimson, black volcanic rock. Monumental. Brutal. Cinematic.
- **Shadow of the Colossus** — melancholy vast emptiness. Ancient things that were never evil. Just enormous and in the way.
- **SAW Universe** — industrial dungeon. You made the choices. Now live with them. The doll is watching.
